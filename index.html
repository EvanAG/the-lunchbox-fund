The Lunchbox Fund

Fostering education via nutrition, The Lunchbox Fund is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing a daily meal for orphaned and vulnerable school children in township and rural areas of South Africa, a country where 65% of all children live in poverty.

Where We Give

Donate

There are countless children in need of a proper meal and with your help they can experience healthy and nutritious food. In South Africa, 56% of all children live in poverty, which is over 12 million children below the poverty line with 3.6 million orphans among them. It starts with one.
